JC Video Factory Terms of Service

Last updated: June 5, 2026

  • Publish only content you have the right to use.

  • Follow TikTok's Terms of Service, Community Guidelines, and Music Usage Confirmation.
  • Use the TikTok publishing feature only for the account that completed OAuth authorization.

Access to the TikTok publishing feature can be disabled at any time by removing the stored OAuth token from local storage or revoking the app from the TikTok account settings.
