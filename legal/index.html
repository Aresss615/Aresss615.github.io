JC Video Factory

A personal tool for creating short-form videos and publishing them to your own connected accounts.

JC Video Factory is a single-owner application that helps its owner generate short-form videos and post them to their own connected social accounts — including TikTok. The owner writes or selects a story, the tool produces a finished vertical video, and the owner reviews it before choosing to publish to an account they control. It is not a multi-user service and does not post on behalf of anyone but the owner.

Checking creator service…

The creator/publishing service runs on the owner's machine and is only online when they are actively creating. These pages stay available regardless.

What it does

  • Script & narrationGenerates a story script and a TTS voiceover locally.
  • Word-by-word captionsSubtitles highlight in sync with the narration.
  • Relevant b-rollMatches background footage to the script rather than picking at random.
  • Owner-controlled publishingSends the finished video to the owner's own connected TikTok account.

How it works

  1. The owner provides a story or idea on their own machine.
  2. The tool assembles a vertical video: voiceover, captions, and matched background.
  3. The owner previews the result and edits the caption, hashtags, and privacy as desired.
  4. The owner clicks publish to send the video to their own connected account.

How it uses TikTok

JC Video Factory uses TikTok's Content Posting API solely so the owner can publish their own generated videos to their own TikTok account. The owner authorizes the connection once via TikTok Login, and only the video.upload and video.publish scopes are requested. The tool never reads other users' content and never posts to accounts other than the owner's connected account. Until the app is approved by TikTok, posts are created as private (visible only to the owner).
